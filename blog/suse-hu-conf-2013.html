SUSE Conference 2013 @ Budapest

Posted Friday, 28 June 2013 by Miklos
Tags: en libreoffice

Yesterday Novell Hungary organized SUSE Conference 2013 here at Budapest. As you can see above Andras held a LibreOffice-related presentation there — and we also ran a LibreOffie booth. As it was requested, we set up quite some (publicly available) Android demos:

  • Impress remote

  • LibreOffice4Android (document viewer) on my phone (I needed a fix to get it running on a non-tablet, though)

  • The desktop app on a tablet — thanks goes to the organizers who provided that for us!

About 200 visitors attended the conference, which counts as a great success in this category. :-)
